About Hana

I became a photographer after realizing the moments I treasured most were never the “perfect” ones. They were the quiet in-between seconds: a hand squeeze, a soft laugh, a deep breath before a big day. I wanted to preserve those feelings in a way that looks beautiful now and still feels honest years later.

Style & niche

My work leans light and airy with a subtle editorial finish, guided, but never stiff. I specialize in portraits, couples, and intimate events that value authenticity.

FAQs

Do you charge extra for travel?

Travel within central Tokyo is included. Outside Tokyo may require a small travel fee depending on distance.

What if the weather is bad?

We can reschedule once at no charge, or switch to an indoor location that suits your style.

Can I get the RAW files?

I typically deliver edited high-resolution images. RAW files can be provided for an additional fee upon request.
